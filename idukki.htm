IDUKKI

Idukki ia s beautiful high range district of Kerala is geographically known for its Mountainous Hills and Dense Forests. Idukki district lies amid the Western Ghats of Kerala. Idukki is known as the "Spice Garden of Kerala".

Munnar is a hill station located in the Idukki district. Munnar is situated at around 1,600 metres (5,200 ft) above mean sea level, in the Western Ghats mountain range. Munnar is also called the "Kashmir of South India". Munnar has some of the largest tea plantations in the world.

Thekkady is a town near Periyar National Park, an important tourist attraction in the Idukki district. The sanctuary is famous for its dense evergreen, semi-evergreen, moist deciduous forests and savanna grass lands. It is home to herds of elephants, sambar, tigers, gaur, lion-tailed macaques and Nilgiri langurs.
